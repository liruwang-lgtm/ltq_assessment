About This Assessment

This assessment is designed to help you identify where you currently are in your leadership transition journey — and what that position is asking of you.

It contains 18 statements grouped into three sections. Your responses across the three sections will identify your primary transition stage and the dimensions most activated in your current experience.

Instructions: Rate each statement from 1 (Not at all true) to 5 (Very much true) based on your experience over the last 60–90 days. Answer based on your genuine experience — there are no right or wrong answers.
