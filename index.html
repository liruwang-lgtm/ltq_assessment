About This Assessment

Every significant leadership transition — a promotion, a restructure, a career pivot, or a period of growth — moves through three distinct stages: Taking-Off, Cruising, and Awakening. Each stage has its own emotional terrain, its own identity challenges, and its own specific demands on who you are as a leader.

This assessment helps you identify which stage you are primarily in right now — and what that stage is asking of you. There are no right or wrong answers. Answer based on your experience over the last 60–90 days.
